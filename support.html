RetroHangman - Retro Video Game Word Puzzle

Report a Bug or Issue

If you've found a bug or are having trouble with RetroHangman, please open an issue on our GitHub repository:

github.com/tgrable/RetroHangman/issues

When reporting an issue, it helps to include:

  • What happened and what you expected to happen
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Steps to reproduce the problem

Frequently Asked Questions

Does RetroHangman collect my personal data?

No. RetroHangman does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (including high scores) is stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does RetroHangman contain ads?

No. RetroHangman does not display ads or use any advertising services.

Does RetroHangman require an internet connection?

No. The game works fully offline with no network calls.
